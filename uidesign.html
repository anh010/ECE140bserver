UI design examples

A good example of Hicks Law is the paymobile UI, when you open the mobile app you are presented with the option to send, or request money. Which is likely the reason you open the app. Receiving money is reserved for the top of the screen when applicaple.

Example of Law of proximity, In photoshop there are many options to select or highlight an area of a photo. But they are all within click distance from each other. Marquee tool, Lasso and Magic wand/quick select

Example of Doherty Threshold, would be Visual Studio Code, when interacting with you never feel like you have to wait for the program, its fast and responsive. More specificaly is the Intellisense, its so quick and handy that it keeps productivity up.
